NFT Play to Earn Battle Simulator Where Heroes Battle for METAL
Exclusive on ICON, Code Metal is a battle-to-earn, on-chain battle simulator, with a unique collection of 4999 Hero NFTs built on the ICON blockchain. It`s the first game that Upgradable NFTs are used in a Battle Simulator P2E game.
Sale
4999 Hero NFTs can be minted with $ICX. Gen 0 Heroes will cost 150 $ICX for whitelist and 200 $ICX in public sale.
Upgrade
Higher level Heroes earn more rewards. Higher levels are increasingly harder to reach, as the amount of $METAL you need to burn to purchase the next level increases. This not only increases the battle level of your Heroes, but their desirability too, as it permanently upgrades their on-chain metadata.
Battle-to-Earn Game-Ready
To play the game, you need to mint at least one Hero NFT. One Hero can target exactly one enemy. On every kill enemies drop rewards, rewards differ based on the strength of the enemy. Choose your battles wisely!
